B.Tech CSE AI & ML

Overview

Computer Science Engineering is a promising career choice for those who are interested in technology and its applications. As a computer science engineer, you will work on designing, developing and maintaining computer systems and software applications that help organizations and individuals to solve real-world problems. To succeed in this field, you will need to have a strong analytical and problem-solving mindset, as well as the ability to work collaboratively in a team environment. You should also be passionate about learning new technologies and staying up-to-date with the latest industry trends and best practices.

Career Prospective

Career in computer science engineering, you need to obtain a degree in computer science or a related field. This will equip you with the foundational knowledge and skills needed to work in this field. Some common areas of study include programming languages, algorithms, data structures, operating systems, computer networks, database management, and software engineering. Once you have a degree, you can apply for various job opportunities in software development, web development, database management, cybersecurity, artificial intelligence, machine learning, and other related areas. As a computer science engineer, you can work in a variety of industries, such as IT, healthcare, finance, gaming, and more.

Eligibility

Applicants must have passed the 10+2 examination with Physics and Mathematics as compulsory subjects and Chemistry / Computer Science. With at least 50% marks in the above subjects, taken together and 50% marks overall.

STUDENT JOURNEY

The educational prospects stemming from this program are limitless. You have the potential to sculpt your career trajectory in AI and ML-driven domains, including data analysis, machine learning engineering, natural language processing, computer vision, and more.

With a huge explosion in data and its applications, a career in the field of AI&ML can be very promising as Big Data Engineer, Business Intelligence Developer, Data Scientist, Machine Learning Engineer, Research Scientist, AI Data Analyst, AI Engineer, Robotics Scientist, etc.

JIT B Tech CSE (AI & ML) Program Educational Objectives

Upon completing our Computer Science Engineering program with a focus on Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML), students will be equipped to:

  • Attain leadership roles within their organizations, leveraging their technical expertise in AI and ML to manage complex projects and teams effectively.
  • Innovate and develop sustainable AI and ML solutions to intricate technological challenges, contributing to the advancement of these cutting-edge fields.
  • Demonstrate emotional intelligence and effective teamwork skills when collaborating with diverse, cross-cultural teams in the global tech landscape, particularly in AI and ML-driven projects.
  • Commit to a lifelong journey of continuous learning, adapting to the ever-evolving world of AI, ML, and technology, ensuring their expertise remains at the forefront of industry developments.
  • Foster an entrepreneurial mindset, enabling them to identify and capitalize on opportunities in the dynamic AI and ML industry, including areas like AI-driven startups and innovative tech ventures.
  • Efficiently harness cutting-edge AI and ML technology, applying it creatively to address real-world problems, automate processes, and drive innovation across various sectors.
  • Uphold the highest ethical standards in their AI and ML work, promoting integrity, responsible AI usage, and social consciousness within the rapidly evolving landscape of artificial intelligence and machine learning.
