College of Engineering

The College of Pharmacy is established in the year 2019, with a goal to impart quality education, skills, knowledge. The Pharmaceutical Science is a rapidly growing field in India. New technology is emerging day by day globally in the field of Pharmaceutical Science so there is always an increasing demand for highly qualified professionals for quality education and research institutions for producing highly trained pharmacists as per the need of Pharmaceutical Industry, Hospitals and in Community. The College of Pharmacy offers education for Bachelors (B. Pharm) and Diploma Program in Pharmacy. We have highly dedicated, qualified, enthusiastic and exceptionally brilliant faculty to ensure betterment of students in every aspect and to uplift students of rural areas.

B.Tech is regarded as the most preferred course choice among the science stream students. JIT has engineering department which serves in the field of technical education as one of the best B.Tech college in Lucknow & Barabanki area. Engineering is an ultimate field for research and innovation and it is certainly quite popular among the students. JIT known to have the top B.Tech college in Lucknow & Barabanki area aims at making their engineering students industry ready professionals so that they are able to be absorbed by the best organizations in their respective domain. The college has qualified faculties who uses unique teaching techniques to empower the student’s education. Dedicated labs, as well as forward-looking curriculum, helps students to excel in their field of engineering. JIT, in general, has five departments in B.Tech such as Mechanical, Electrical, Electronics and Communication, Computer Science and Civil which are considered as the backbone of engineering. JIT also provides extra curriculum activities so that overall development for the students can take place. This also includes personality development as well as blooming of one’s character.
