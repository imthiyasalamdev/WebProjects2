Mission Statement:

“JIT mission is to provide educational experiences that empower our students to become lifelong learners, leaders, and contributors to the society. JIT is committed to fostering a diverse and inclusive community that values academic excellence, innovation, and service to others. JIT colleges are dedicated in preparing students to thrive in a rapidly changing world by providing them the knowledge, skills, and opportunities they need to succeed”.

Vision Statement:

“JIT vision is to be a leading institution of higher education, recognized for its academic excellence, innovation, and commitment to social responsibility. JIT is creating a vibrant, inclusive community that inspires creativity, critical thinking, and a passion for lifelong learning. JIT colleges are dedicated in promoting intellectual curiosity, scientific inquiry, and artistic expression, as well as ethical values, social justice, and environmental sustainability. JIT prepares its students to be leaders in their fields, equipped them to solve complex problems, adapt to new challenges, and enable them to make a positive impact to their society & the world”.

Core Values:

To achieve our mission and vision, we are guided by the following core values:

  1. Academic Excellence: We strive to provide a rigorous, challenging, and engaging educational experience that prepares our students for success in their chosen fields.
  2. Innovation: We foster a culture of innovation, creativity, and experimentation that encourages new ideas, approaches, and solutions.
  3. Inclusivity: We embrace diversity and inclusivity in all aspects of our community, and we are committed to creating a safe, welcoming, and respectful environment for all.
